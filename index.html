Fighting Burnout

Get your life back.

We're so tired of being enslaved to the EHR
Physician burnout? Cue the EHRs. Doctors used to juggle stethoscopes and symptoms, not clicks and checkboxes. Now, ‘M.D.’ feels like it stands for 'Mentally Drained'. In the world of endless data entry, the white coat is losing its luster, one click at a time.
Reclaim your Time

4.5 hours a day

That's what we waste clicking in our EHR
Recent studies paint a dismal picture, revealing that doctors spend an alarming two-thirds of their time navigating through the labyrinth of EHRs. This equates to countless hours of meticulous data entry, clicking boxes, and wrestling with unresponsive interfaces—a colossal waste of expertise and a stark diversion from patient-centered care.
Look at your Patients

There was a time

when clinicians could look at their patients
Remember eye contact? Neither do we! Thanks to EHRs, doctors are perfecting the art of a warm, compassionate gaze... into their computer screens. Patients are getting diagnoses, sure, but they’re also getting a great view of their doctor’s hairline. It's like a weird game of hide and seek where the doc hides behind a screen, and genuine human connection is what’s seeking!

Our mission is to rediscover the soul of medicine.

Strip away the digital noise and bringing a breath of fresh humanity back into healthcare.
