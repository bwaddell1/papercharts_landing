Hipaa and Security

Last updated: 10/15/2023

Our committment to security

At PaperCharts.ai, we hold the privacy and security of your data in the highest regard. Navigating the complex landscape of healthcare information requires advanced, tailored solutions, and we’re proud to be at the forefront of innovative, secure, and compliant data management. We adopt a multifaceted approach to ensure the sanctity and confidentiality of Protected Health Information (PHI) is uncompromised.

Enhanced Data Encryption

Leveraging the robust capabilities of Amazon Web Services (AWS), we employ state-of-the-art encryption methodologies that ensure your data is secure at every touchpoint. Data in transit and at rest is encased in multiple layers of encryption, making unauthorized access nearly impossible.

Access Management

Our dynamic access controls are defined by precision and adaptability. We’ve established intricate authorization protocols that permit access based on specific roles and permissions, ensuring that every piece of information is accessible only to those who need it, and always under strict supervision.

Infrastructure Security

Hosted on AWS, our application benefits from the world’s most secure and reliable cloud infrastructure. Coupled with our own enhanced security measures, including constant monitoring and advanced analytics to ensure the safety and integrity of your data.

Continuous Security Assessments

We’ve institutionalized a culture of continuous improvement, characterized by ongoing audits and assessments. This proactive approach ensures that our security posture is not only robust but also evolving, capable of countering emerging threats with efficiency and precision.

Empowering Our Team

Every member of the PaperCharts.ai team undergoes rigorous training, evolving their understanding of data security, privacy protocols, and HIPAA regulations. We believe in empowering our team with knowledge, making them the first and most effective line of defense against security threats.

Proactive Incident Management

We are prepared to identify, contain, and mitigate any security threat, ensuring minimal impact and swift recovery. Every incident is a learning opportunity, enabling us to fortify our defenses for the future.

Data Backup and Recovery

With AWS at the core, our backup and recovery solutions are characterized by reliability and speed. Every piece of data is backed up systematically, ensuring that in the event of an unforeseen incident, data integrity and availability are uncompromised.

Vendor and Partner Screening

We collaborate with partners who share our commitment to data security. Each associate undergoes a thorough vetting process, and we continuously monitor these collaborations to ensure that your data’s security is never compromised.

De-Identification of Data

At PaperCharts.ai, we adopt a proactive approach to privacy by de-identifying data whenever possible. This practice ensures enhanced privacy, allowing us to utilize data for insights while eliminating risks associated with sensitive information.

Compliance Excellence

Our adherence to HIPAA and other regulatory standards is unwavering. We’re not just compliant; we aim to exceed the statutory requirements, offering our clients the peace of mind that comes with knowing their data is handled with the utmost care and professionalism.

PaperCharts.ai is more than a data management application; we’re your partners in ensuring that sensitive healthcare data is accorded the respect, privacy, and security it deserves. For further details or any inquiries, connect with us at security@papercharts.ai. We’re here to ensure your data security is uncompromised and unparalleled.
