Common Questions

Not quite convinced?

Is there a discount for annual billing?
Indie pricing is for independant or small group practices. We do volume discounts for larger groups. Get in touch to find out more
Is the exported data portable among other apps?
We are working to add API integrations for as many EHR vendors as we can. Some are set up to do this quickly, others don't play so well. If your vendor is not yet listed then you can copy/paste the results into your note in the meantime. Each EHR vendor's API is different as well, some will not allow us to send data into an encounter note so the integrations will be limited.
Is this suitable for a full team of people?
Yes, we only charge per clinician seat. We hope you will utilize your support staff to print and scan the notes and load them into your EHR system to help save you time. Its our goal to return to the glory days where you pick up your chart outside the room, and drop it off complete! 
Is private data shared with third parties or advertisers?
We de-identify patient data scanned from your notes, and send it to an API to help generate readable text for your note. No private data is shared with third parties or advertisers
